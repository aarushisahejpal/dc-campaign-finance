About this data

This tracker draws from three filing systems that are not otherwise linked: the OCF e-filing portal for traditional candidates, PACs, and independent expenditure committees; the DC Fair Elections Program for publicly financed candidates; and the Federal Election Commission for the DC Delegate race.

Traditional candidate data reflects filings through the March 10, 2026 R&E report. Fair Elections candidates file monthly; their individual contribution data comes from the Fair Elections public API. FEC data is updated daily via the FEC API. The next major filing deadline is June 10, 2026.
